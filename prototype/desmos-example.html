Figure 1: Supply and Demand Lead to Equilibrium

Ⓐ The demand curve shows the quantity that buyers plan to buy at each price.

Ⓑ The supply curve shows the quantity that sellers plan to sell, at each price.

Ⓒ Equilibrium occurs at the point where the quantity buyers demand is equal to the quantity that sellers supply.

Ⓓ The equilibrium occurs at a price of $3.

Ⓔ The quantity bought and sold in this equilibrium is 100 gallons.
